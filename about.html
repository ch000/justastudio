About Studio

Just a studio represents the intersection of design, culture, and art, forging an effective visual language characterized by bold aesthetics and engaging storytelling. With a focus on visual design, innovative web design, and captivating motion images, Just a studio stands as a testament to innovation and versatility in creative expression. A approach is rooted in a deep understanding of artistic principles, blended seamlessly with modern technology to deliver unique, impactful design solutions that resonate across diverse mediums and audiences.

About Lei

She began her design journey with a Master's degree from Cheung Kong School of Art and Design at Shantou University, followed by further specialization at Bauhaus University. Her professional path spans Shanghai, Weimar, and Dresden, leaving her mark on various design projects. Driven by a passion for culture, art, and design, her work blends innovative solutions with cultural understanding. As a freelancer, she has worked with diverse clients in Germany and China, exploring the intersection of creative expression and practical design.
