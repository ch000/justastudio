Studierendenwerk Berlin

Against the backdrop of the pandemic era, students from the Berlin University of the Arts have initiated and organized an online exhibition. This art collective comprises 10 artists from various countries and cultural backgrounds, showcasing photography, sound installations, video installations, and more. The exhibition is scheduled to take place from April 30th to May 2nd, 2021, in cooperation with KUNSTRAUM POTSDAMER STRASSE.‍‍
